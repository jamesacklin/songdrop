Track Summon

A companion for a music library you host yourself.

◎
Connect to your server
This app talks only to your Track Summon server. It has no catalog of its own and does nothing until it's connected.
⌕
Search and request
Look up a track and send a request. Your server finds it, tags it, and files it into Plex.
✓
Your library, your rules
Organize music you own and manage playlists. You're responsible for what you add and for respecting copyright.
Connect

Away from home, reach your server over Tailscale, WireGuard, or an HTTPS reverse proxy.
